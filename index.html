The Perfect PB & J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sanwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Any peanut butter and jam will work but my recommendation is to use grape jelly and all natural peanut butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slice of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural peanut butter

Directions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly on the other side.
  4. Put the two slices together. Enjoy!!
